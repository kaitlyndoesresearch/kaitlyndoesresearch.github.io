About Me

I am a second-year student in the Human-Computer Interaction program at Iowa State University; I aim to receive my Ph.D. in May of 2022. User Experience (UX) and Computer Supported Cooperative Work (CSCW) are my favorite topics. I aim to continue researching teams, teamwork, and user experience.

As a user researcher, the skill set I earned as a motivated psychology student directly impacts the studies I conduct. To date, I have crafted eleven surveys and led six semi-structured interviews. I implemented these tools in all nine studies I have been involved with, seven of which are efforts I led. I am also experienced in creating observational note sheets and in gathering design requirements.
